About

Business-first thinking.
Built on data.

I'm a digital strategy and performance leader based in Melbourne. My work sits at the intersection of commercial strategy, brand thinking, and performance marketing: translating business objectives into channel strategies that actually move the needle.

I don't start with platforms. I start with where a business needs to go: its revenue targets, competitive position, and customer journey. From there, I build the cross-channel investment strategy to get it there. At Impressive Digital Agency, that approach has driven $430K in new ARR, a 93% pitch win rate, and consistently outsized results across a portfolio of 19 clients.

Alongside client work, I operate as a strategic partner to agency leadership: leading Quarterly Business Reviews, contributing to commercial conversations with agency partners, shaping internal R&D direction, and building the systems and frameworks that make the broader team more effective. I'm as comfortable in a boardroom as I am in a dashboard.
